The Brew Crew Podcast

by: Jordan, Maddy, & Megan

About:

Welcome to The Brew Crew Podcast. Join Maddy, Megan, and Jordan as they talk with the owners, managers, and bartenders at the five breweries in Harrisonburg, Virginia participating in the Shenandoah Beerwerks Trail. Learn about their most memorable moment, most popular beers, how they got involved with the Beerwerks Trail, and more!
